About Me

Hello! I'm Aaryan Kainth, a senior studying Mechanical Engineering at the University of California, Santa Barbara. I enjoy mechanical design, climbing, and long walks on the beach, but above all else, I like to make things.

Resume

I've had the chance to do some really awesome work at some really awesome places - here are a few of them.

Experience

Walt Disney Imagineering Research & Development

Lab Associate

June 2025 - September 2025

At WDI R&D, I worked on the next generation of autonomous aquatic performance robots.

I designed and made parts, tooling, and test equipment, leveraging SolidWorks, a variety of 3D printing techniques, laser and waterjet cutting, and speed rail.

Hawkes Lab

Undergraduate Research Assistant

May 2023 - Present

Working in the Hawkes Lab was what first got me interested in robotics research. During my time here, I've mostly worked on a retractable anchor launcher that lets extraterrestrial rovers rescue themselves when stuck.

I designed the anchor and its clutch mechanism, and first authored the paper, gaining experience in mechanical design, experimental design, and physics modeling.

Plume Design, Inc.

Hardware Team Summer Intern

June 2021 - August 2021

Interning at Plume showed me a little bit of what electrical engineering is all about, and gave me a surface level primer on antenna design.

In my time there, I primarily worked on testing, writing scripts in Python for 3 different kinds of product antennas, and using a Raspberry Pi to collect data for a wall mounted fall-detection prototype.
